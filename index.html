Zsuzsanna Bojta Mezzo-Soprano

Being a mezzo-soprano, Zsuzsanna Bojta started her career as a concert-singer. She has sung the alto solos of almost all the frequently played oratorios, such as Bach Passions, Cantatas, Magnificat, Mozart Masses, Requiem, Handel Messiah, Rossini Stabat mater, Dvorak Requiem etc.

Her operatic career started in 2008 with Mozart Le Nozze di Figaro, as Marcellina.

She was on maternity leave for almost nine years, and last year she started to rebuild her life as a singer again.

In 2016, she sung 2nd Lady in Mozart's Magic Flute in Germany (Heilbronn, Stuttgart, Augsburg, Mannheim), and sung Berenice (leading role) in Mancini's Gl'amanti generosi in 2017, FUGA baroque Festival.

She adores trouser roles, such as Cherubino, Octavian, Siebel, Romeo, and is also familiar with the roles of Dorabella, Carmen, even Delilah. She has just started to widen her repertoire with Wagner roles such as Fricka and Erda.
